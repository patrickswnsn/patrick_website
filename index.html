Case Study: Austrian Public Broadcasting (ORF)

+

From 2013 to 2023, I led digital innovation at Austria's largest newsroom. As Head of Social Media for the news brand Zeit im Bild, I built a team that transformed how young audiences consume news in Austria, reaching 2.4 million followers in a country of 9 million people.

The Challenge

In 2013, Austria's leading news program faced a critical challenge: while very popular on TV, we were becoming invisible to viewers under 35. My mission was to transform Zeit im Bild for the social media age — without compromising the rigorous journalism that made us the country's most trusted news source.

The Approach

  • Built and led a team of six digital journalists, fostering a culture of experimentation
  • Developed new formats in vertical video optimized for each platform
  • Created a talent-driven video strategy that resonates with GenZ
  • Used data analytics to inform editorial decisions and product development
  • Started exploring the use of AI for content moderation in 2017
