What We Do

  • Design and build AI tools for media organizations
  • Train editors and reporters on AI implementation
  • Help leaders develop AI strategies for their organizations

How We Got Started

Our work is shaped by our time at Stanford University as John S. Knight fellows, where we explored how artificial intelligence is impacting journalism and the information ecosystem. This research led to the founding of Verso.

Verso's projects combine technical innovation with deep newsroom experience to tackle two key challenges: helping journalists work effectively with AI, and understanding how information spreads in today's fragmented media landscape.

From 2013 to 2023, I worked on digital innovation at Austria's largest newsroom. As Head of Social Media for the news brand Zeit im Bild, I built a team that transformed how young audiences consume news in Austria, reaching 2.4 million followers in a country of 9 million people.

The Challenge

In 2013, Austria's leading news program faced a critical challenge: while very popular on TV, it was becoming invisible to viewers under 35. My mission was to transform Zeit im Bild for the social media age - without compromising the rigorous journalism that made it the country's most trusted news source.

The Approach

  • Built and led a team of six digital journalists, fostering a culture of experimentation
  • Developed new formats in vertical video optimized for social platforms
  • Created a talent-driven video strategy that resonates with GenZ
  • Used data analytics to inform editorial decisions and product development
  • Started exploring the use of AI for content moderation in 2017
